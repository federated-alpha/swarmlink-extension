# Privacy Policy

**SwarmLink by Federated Alpha**
Last updated: February 10, 2026

## What We Collect

**Nothing beyond what you explicitly provide.**

- Your connected wallet address (sent from our website, not extracted from your browser)
- Swarm membership (which hives you join/create)
- Token scan requests (which tokens you scan)

## What We Don't Collect

- No browsing history
- No keystrokes or clipboard data
- No cookies from other sites
- No personal information (name, email, IP)
- No analytics or telemetry
- No data from pages you visit (we only read token addresses from URLs)

## Where Data Lives

| Data | Location | Synced? |
|------|----------|---------|
| Your swarms | `chrome.storage.local` (your device) | No |
| Theme preference | `chrome.storage.sync` (your Chrome profile) | Across your devices only |
| Wallet address | `chrome.storage.local` (your device) | No |

## API Calls

The extension communicates only with `https://federatedalpha.com/api` to:
- Scan tokens for rug risk
- Submit sentiment signals to your swarms
- Fetch your swarm list

No data is sent to any other server.

## Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this file and note it in the release changelog.
